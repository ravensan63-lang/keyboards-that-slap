# Switch guide

## Linear (smooth)
- Red — light, quiet, fast. Good for gaming.
- Yellow — even lighter spring. Popular for speed.

## Tactile (bump)
- Brown — light bump. Jack of all trades.
- Holy Pandas — satisfying thock, heavier bump.

## Clicky (loud)
- Blue — classic click. Annoying to coworkers.
- Green — heavier blue. Very loud.

## Tip
Buy a switch tester before committing to 100 switches.
